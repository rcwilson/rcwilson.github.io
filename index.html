Journey

• • •

I moved from a small town in Ohio to Columbus and have called it home for 10 years.

Having years of service-industry experience, it was natural to find my calling as a bartender for the majority of my life in the city. It's an exciting life of crafting and making connections with people.

In Fall '19, I decided to enroll in the coding bootcamp We Can Code It to pursue a career role in technology. Taking a leap to the professional and technical world is one of my favorite decisions I've ever made.

Bring It

• • •

The ultimate party planner! Users can log in and create or check an event, then claim items needed or create their own. Bring it is about to be a potluck savior.

The data is stored in a cloud database called Mongoose. Bring It works through a custom-built API that handles the logic and management of that data.
